HOW IT WORKS

The KRY bracelet was purposefully designed to be smart yet inconspicuous. With these three basic functions, KRY helps to offer its user some peace of mind.

The KRY bracelet is worn prior to calibrate the system and take note of the user's normal heart rate.

Equipped with a recess button with fingerprint sensor to mute the alert in the case of a false alarm.

When the bracelet detects a spike in heart rate indicative of panic, then the bracelet will be activated.

FEATURES

HEART RATE MONITOR

A sensor placed along the inside of the bracelet detects a key physiological response to fear: increased heart rate.

MOBILE APP

The KRY bracelet is accompanied by a smartphone app to help you make the call when you can't.

FINGERPRINT DEACTIVATION

False alarm? No need to fret. Just use your fingerprint for simple and secure deactivation.

LED LIGHTS

The bracelet is equipped with lights that turn on upon activation.

GPS LOCATION

The bracelet comes equipped with GPS location. Upon activation, local first responders are notified and dispatched to the user's whereabouts.
