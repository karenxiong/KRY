ABOUT US

The public sphere can still be a fraught and frightening place for women to navigate - especially alone and especially at night. We aim to change that.

The KRY bracelet is a simple device with a big mission.

Co-founders Karen Xiong, Rachel Chin, and Yi Shi leveraged their skills, resources, and energy to create a personal alarm system that is sleek, stylish, and discreet.

KRY is focused on developing solutions to help make a difference. We created the bracelet with the belief that it can redefine the status quo - and the belief that it is possible to create a safer future.

HOW IT WORKS

The KRY bracelet was purposefully designed to be smart yet inconspicuous.
