The KRY bracelet was purposefully designed to be smart yet inconspicuous. With just three basic functions, KRY helps to give our users some peace of mind.

The KRY bracelet is worn before usage in order to calibrate the system and take note of your normal heart rate.

Equipped with a recess button with a fingerprint sensor, this bracelet can be muted anytime and anywhere in the case of a false alarm.

When your heart rate spikes, often due to panic or fear, the bracelet will be activated as soon as it's detected.

FEATURES

FINGERPRINT DEACTIVATION

False alarm? No need to fret. Just use your fingerprint for simple and secure deactivation.

MOBILE APP

The KRY bracelet is accompanied by a smartphone app to help you make the call when you can't.

HEART RATE MONITOR

A sensor placed along the inside of the bracelet detects a key physiological response to fear: increased heart rate.

LED LIGHTS

The bracelet is equipped with lights that turn on upon activation.

GPS LOCATION

The bracelet comes equipped with GPS location. Upon activation, local first responders are notified and dispatched to the user's whereabouts.
